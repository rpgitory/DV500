1 What is Amazon EBS volume?
Ans. For increasing storage capacity on E2C instance by adding hdd or ssd storage drives.
	
2 EBS stands for?
Ans. Amazon elastic block store 
	
3 How to create EBS volume?
Ans. In configure instance in EC2 instance, click on add storage then you can either choose HDD or SSD storage 
	
4 Where we can add EBS volume?
Ans. in EC2 instance click on volume and then add a volume 
	
5 What are the categories of EBS volume?
Ans. HDD and SSD (general SSD, Provisioned IOPS SSD, cold HDD, throughout optimized HDD, magnetic)
	
6 SSD stands for?
Ans. solid state drive
	
7 HDD stands for?
Ans. hard disk drive
	
8 What are the advantages of SSD over HDD?
Ans. ssd is much much faster then hdd bcz there is no moving parts unlike hdd does (platter, hand-ark and so on)
	
9 What are the advantages of HDD?
Ans. affordable price for more storage.
